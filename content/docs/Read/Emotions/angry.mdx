  <div style={{
    fontSize: "1rem",
    fontWeight: "normal",
    textAlign: "left",
    marginTop: "1rem"
  }}>Rabbi aʿūdhu bika min hamazāti-sh-shayāṭīn, wa aʿūdhu bika Rabbi ay-yaḥḍurūn.</div>

  <div style={{
    fontSize: "1rem",
    fontWeight: "normal",
    textAlign: "left",
    marginTop: "1rem"
  }}>My Lord, I seek protection with You from the promptings of the devils; and I seek protection in You, my Lord, from their coming near me.</div>

  <div style={{

### **4. The Ultimate Duʿa**

<div style={{
  textAlign: "right",
  lineHeight: "2",
  fontSize: "1.5rem",
  color: "#ffffff",
  backgroundColor: "#1a1a1a",
  padding: "1rem",
  borderRadius: "10px"
}}>
  <div style={{
    fontFamily: "'Amiri', serif",
    fontSize: "1.5rem"
  }}>
    اللَّهُمَّ بِعِلْمِكَ الْغَيْبَ وَقُدْرَتِكَ عَلَى الْخَلْقِ، أَحْيِنِي مَا عَلِمْتَ الْحَيَاةَ خَيْرًا لِي، 
    وَتَوَفَّنِي إِذَا عَلِمْتَ الْوَفَاةَ خَيْرًا لِي، اللَّهُمَّ وَأَسْأَلُكَ خَشْيَتَكَ فِي الْغَيْبِ 
    وَالشَّهَادَةِ، وَأَسْأَلُكَ كَلِمَةَ الْحَقِّ فِي الرِّضَا وَالْغَضَبِ، وَأَسْأَلُكَ الْقَصْدَ فِي 
    الْفَقْرِ وَالْغِنَى، وَأَسْأَلُكَ نَعِيمًا لَا يَنْفَدُ، وَأَسْأَلُكَ قُرَّةَ عَيْنٍ لَا تَنْقَطِعُ، 
    وَأَسْأَلُكَ الرِّضَا بَعْدَ الْقَضَاءِ، وَأَسْأَلُكَ بَرْدَ الْعَيْشِ بَعْدَ الْمَوْتِ، وَأَسْأَلُكَ 
    لَذَّةَ النَّظَرِ إِلَى وَجْهِكَ، وَالشَّوْقَ إِلَى لِقَائِكَ، فِي غَيْرِ ضَرَّاءَ مُضِرَّةٍ، وَلَا 
    فِتْنَةٍ مُضِلَّةٍ، اللَّهُمَّ زَيِّنَّا بِزِينَةِ الْإِيمَانِ، وَاجْعَلْنَا هُدَاةً مُهْتَدِينَ.
  </div>

  <div style={{
    fontSize: "1rem",
    fontWeight: "normal",
    textAlign: "left",
    marginTop: "1rem"
  }}>
    Allahumma bi-‘ilmikal-ghayb, wa qudratika ‘ala-l-khalq, ahyini ma ‘alimta-l-hayata khayra li, wa tawaffani idha 
    ‘alimta-l-wafata khayra li. Allahumma wa as’aluka khashyataka fi-l-ghayb wa-sh-shahadah, wa as’aluka kalimata-l-haqq 
    fi-r-rida wa-l-ghadab, wa as’aluka al-qasda fi-l-faqri wa-l-ghina, wa as’aluka na‘iman la yanfad, wa as’aluka qurrata 
    ‘aynin la tanqata‘, wa as’aluka ar-rida ba‘da-l-qada’, wa as’aluka barda-l-‘ayshi ba‘da-l-mawt, wa as’aluka ladhdhata 
    an-nazari ila wajhika, wa-sh-shawqa ila liqa’ika, fi ghayri darra’a mudirrah, wa la fitnatin mudillah. Allahumma zayyinna 
    bi-zinati-l-iman, wa-j‘alna hudatan muhtadin.
  </div>

  <div style={{
    fontSize: "1rem",
    fontWeight: "normal",
    textAlign: "left",
    marginTop: "1rem"
  }}>
    O Allah, with Your knowledge of the unseen and Your absolute power over the creation, let me live as long as life 
    is good for me; and give me death when You know death is better for me. O Allah, I ask You for Your fear in private 
    and in public, and for the word of truth in times of joy and anger. I ask You for moderation in poverty and wealth. 
    I ask You for endless blessings and perpetual delights. I ask You to make me pleased with destiny; for a cool and 
    comfortable life after death; for the pleasure of seeing Your Face; and for the longing to meet You, without any 
    painful ordeals and tribulations that misguide. O Allah, adorn us with the beauty of faith, and make us those who 
    guide others and are guided themselves.
  </div>

  <div style={{
    fontStyle: "italic",
    color: "#d1d1d1",
    marginTop: "1.5rem",
    fontSize: "1rem",
    textAlign: "left",
    fontFamily: "'Amiri', serif",
    lineHeight: "1.8"
  }}>
    <b>✨ Hadith Reference:</b> ʿAṭā b. As-Sāʾib narrated: "ʿAmmār b. Yāsir (raḍiy Allāhu ʿanhū) led us in prayer and made it brief. 
    Some said: 'You made the prayer brief.' He replied: 'I recited the supplications I heard from the Messenger 
    of Allah ﷺ.'” (Ibn Mājah 3843)
  </div>
